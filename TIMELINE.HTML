Wonder Woman


Wonder WomanPatty Jenkins' acclaimed Wonder Woman takes us back a full century to Princess Diana of Themyscira's first outing as the titular superhero. After meeting World War I spy Steve Trevor, she leaves her all-female paradise home forever to fight in the Great War against the god Ares. Watch Wonder Woman on Amazon Prime When the film concludes, Wonder Woman has realised that she can't simply fight all the world's problems and goes underground, which explains why she wasn't around when Superman came on the scene.

Wonder Woman 1984


We haven't seen Jenkins' Wonder Woman sequel yet (it's not out until June 2020), but the title pretty much gives the game away. Princess Diana will be back battling the villainous Cheetah against the backdrop of the Cold War. Though from the first trailer, it doesn't seem like the tensions of the Cold War are going to have much to do with Wonder Woman 1984 at all. Instead it looks like the movie will lean on the comic's plot surrounding Maxwell Lord and Brother Eye.

Man of Steel


Clark Kent coming out to the world as Superman in Zack Snyder's Man of Steel kicked off the modern age of superheroes in Worlds of DC's version of Earth (everyone having forgotten Wonder Woman beating up all those German soldiers, apparently). Watch Man of Steel on Now TV The film charts Kal-El's first adventure, fighting General Zod and his invading Kryptonian forces, and ends with Clark taking up his iconic role as a reporter at Metropolis' Daily Planet.

Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice


Zack Snyder's Man of Steel follow-up depicts the first meeting between Ben Affleck's middle-aged Batman and Henry Cavill's Superman, with a bit of Lex Luthor and Doomsday thrown in for good measure. Watch Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice on Now TV Bruce Wayne and Clark begin as enemies, but manage to make it up just in time for Superman to be 'killed' by Doomsday. Diana also resumes her public role as Wonder Woman after Batman catches her attempting to acquire a photograph from World War I in which she appears, and future Justice Leaguers Aquaman, Cyborg and The Flash also have brief cameos.

Justice League


Batman, Wonder Woman, Aquaman, Cyborg and The Flash unite for the first time to battle Steppenwolf, an alien warlord attempting to transform the Earth and destroy all life via a tour of Atlantis, Themyscira and Metropolis to acquire the three 'Mother Boxes'. Superman is resurrected and joins them for the final showdown. Watch Justice League on Amazon Prime The film includes plenty of setup for future movies: Aquaman meets Mera for the first time, Barry Allen (aka Flash) gets a job in Central City's police department, Lex Luthor and the mercenary Deathstroke decide to set up their own evil league, and the presence of the villain Darkseid is hinted at..

Aquaman


While James Wan's Aquaman is kept largely separate from the other goings-on in the Worlds of DC, it does follow up on Arthur Curry's first meeting with Mera. Watch Aquaman on Now TV The movie enlists Aquaman in a quest to prevent a war between the underwater armies of Atlantis and the sea-polluting surface dwellers. If you want to know how it ends, well, star Jason Momoa spoiled it ages before the film came out.
